*Interpretation:* The placebo test assigns a pseudo-treatment year of 2010 (three years before the actual 2013 IFRS mandate). The interaction coefficient is economically small (−0.312) and statistically indistinguishable from zero (*p* = 0.866), supporting the identifying assumption that treated and control firms followed parallel pre-trends in EMI prior to the actual reform. This null result strengthens causal inference for the main DiD estimate in Panel A.
